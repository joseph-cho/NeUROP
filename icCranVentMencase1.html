Cranium, Ventricles, Meninges

Case 1:

A 26 year old woman with a history of acne was prescribed Retin-A. Six weeks after using Retin-A at twice the recommended dose, she starts to develop headaches which become more severe, and she develops blurriness of vision. The headaches are worse lying down than sitting up. She sees a neurologist who looks in her eyes and immediately admits her to neurologist.
